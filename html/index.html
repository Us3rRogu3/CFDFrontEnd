Content Holmes does amazing things

No more tensions, protect your children online easily. Content Holmes is an AI app with a focus on extreme ease of access and complete security, going to lengths to protect your child.

Features

URL Blocker

A blocker like no other. URL Blocker ensures no explicit content ever reaches your children. Simple and safe.

Sentiment Analyzer

Websites that children visit daily tell a lot about their mental health. The power of sentiment analysis allows Holmes to notify you if anything may be wrong. No worries anymore.

Session Timers and Remote Blocking

Internet time limits productivity during exams, and internet is full of distractions. And if you feel so, just ask Content Holmes and he automagically helps you block or restrict access to the internet, right from FB messenger. Ultra uber fast.









Word Replacer

Word replacer intelligently replaces profane words, so that the child can never be affected by them. Over 200 words replaced on the fly helps Holmes achieve this. New and Unique.

Your Personal Child Assistant

Have all power of AI on the tips of your hands. Content Holmes is just like your companion. You can talk to him like you talk to your comrade next door and he keeps you updated on everything on your children's PCs. Innovation safety combined.

Weekly Reports

Simply login on the site to view weekly reports of your children. Each entry is beautifully graphed and annotated, and we break down all the technical stuff to you. Plain, simple, elegant.
