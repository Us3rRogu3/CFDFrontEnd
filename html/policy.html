Collection and Processing by Content Holmes

We automatically collect the following data only after you login. This is to improve your personal experience with the app.



Uninstallation page feedback

After uninstalling Content Holmes, you may be directed to a webpage that asks for your feedback. The webpage anonymously stores some application and platform related data. If you decide to submit feedback, we will also store it anonymously.


Blocked URLs your child has visited

We store the most recently blocked 5 URLs that have been blocked by the extension for you to view. These can be accessed through the Messenger Bot or from the site after logging in.


Depression Scores from processed content picked up from the screen

The information you see is never stored, however, the sentiment analyser stores numerical scores it calculates from the content viewed for the last 7 days.
